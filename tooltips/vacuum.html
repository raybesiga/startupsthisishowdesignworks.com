Jonathan (Jony) Ive, Senior Vice President of Industrial Design at Apple, is a good friend of our new pal Dieter Rams, and takes inspiration from Braun's minimalist aesthetic and focus on function. The examples to the left illustrate the similarities in design thinking and, in most cases, a homage to fifty years of design innovation by Braun.

“I have always regarded Apple products—and the kind words Jony Ive has said about me and my work—as a compliment,” Rams told the paper. “I am always fascinated when I see the latest Apple products. Apple has managed to achieve what I never achieved: using the power of their products to persuade people to queue to buy them.”2
